Join a winning team at SUSE

SUSE and Rancher thriving together

At the end of 2020, SUSE acquired Rancher Labs, the market leader in Kubernetes management. The combined company brings together a best-in-class Linux operating system, a market-leading Kubernetes management platform, and a host of pioneering edge capabilities that will give enterprises the power to innovate everywhere. SUSE and Rancher, in collaboration with a broad open source community, bring the best of innovation and stability for the enterprise. Together we are bringing the ‘Open’ Back to ‘Open Source’.

SUSE, now with Rancher, offers the industry’s most adaptable Linux operating system and the only open Kubernetes management platform. Together with our Edge solutions, we give customers the ability to innovate fast and transform according to their own priorities.

Why work for SUSE? 

For SUSE, open means more than software. It's a business model and a culture. Our purpose is to give customers the freedom to innovate everywhere. The key to reaching this goal are our employees, partners, community, and customers. There's no limit to what we can achieve through the power of many.

At SUSE, we set challenging goals for ourselves, then work to meet, and often exceed them. How far can you go with extraordinary colleagues at your side?

Opening Our Culture For Your Success 

Open Source is in our DNA. It shapes our unique culture where you are encouraged to be yourself and share your expertise.

We share a passion for technology and market-leading innovation. Our employees are ambitious and strive for excellence. Our people are confident and work within an empowered and flexible environment to achieve our strategic goals.

Our leaders believe in trust and collaboration. They define the path and direction and are on hand to guide and inspire. They believe that everybody has a unique contribution and they foster a culture of learning.

Employee networks and CSR

Our networks are open to all SUSE employees. They provide opportunities for learning, networking, building friendships and
making a difference to SUSE and our communities. These include our Women in Tech Network, Pride at SUSE, and our sustainability-focused group, Go Green.

We also believe in giving back to our community. Every SUSE employee can provide financial donations on SUSE’s behalf to charities around the world, via our philanthropic giving program, SUSEcares, and has one day off a year to volunteer for a charity of their choice. We are also planting one tree for every employee in Madagascar, our beloved chameleon’s natural habitat. 

Choose open and join us!
