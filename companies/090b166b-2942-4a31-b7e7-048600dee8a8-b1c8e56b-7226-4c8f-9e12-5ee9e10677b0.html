Do work that matters – from ideation to implementation

We ideate, design and build innovative digital products, portals, platforms and services that delight your customers so you stand out in the market. Our teams scale digital delivery organisations to rapidly deploy reliable and scalable features and services. With our extensive partner network and start-up ecosystem, we blend off-the-shelf products with custom solutions built for your business alone – and operate them reliably.

 

People & Culture

We are a people business that emphasises quality, reliability and respect. As a privately held company run by engineers, we cultivate a high-trust culture that takes a long-term view. That’s why our staff turnover is lower than most companies in the industry. Our promise:

✅ Culture of collaboration and teamwork

✅ Professional training and development

✅ Strong ethics with a focus on sustainability & inclusion

✅ Sensible work-life balance & family-friendly environment

✅ Freedom to take over responsibility

 

Projects

We offer a variety of challenging projects from new system builds to scaling up digital environments, application modernisation and data analytics. Our clients range from large multinationals to start-ups across a range of industries. Your job will involve working in small interdisciplinary teams and communicating closely with our clients to build tailored, impactful and sustainable solutions that deliver value.

 

Professional development

Your continuous education is essential for us! We know how important life-long and fast learning is, which is why we invest 10% of our turnover in developing skills and services in an agile way – matching your needs and those of the company. We strongly believe in developing one's skills "on the job", supported by an active knowledge exchange through our internal “Topic Teams”. In addition, Zühlke offers a range of complementary training courses tailored to the needs of the company and its employees, as well as annual company-wide camps to learn and explore ideas together.
